Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on your location. During checkout, you'll see the available shipping options for your address.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We also offer financing options through Affirm for qualified customers.

Can I return my purchase if I'm not satisfied?

Absolutely. We offer a 30-day return policy for all products in their original condition. Simply contact our customer service to initiate a return, and we'll provide a prepaid shipping label.

Are the phones unlocked?

All our phones are sold unlocked unless otherwise specified. This means you can use them with any compatible carrier worldwide. Some carrier-specific models may be locked initially but can be unlocked after meeting the carrier's requirements.

Do you offer trade-in options?

Yes! We offer competitive trade-in values for your old devices. You can get an instant quote on our website by answering a few questions about your device's condition.

How do I check my order status?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also check your order status by logging into your account on our website or by contacting our customer service team.
